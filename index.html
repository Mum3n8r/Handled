FAQ
Straight answers.
Do I need technical knowledge to install it?+
You copy one script tag into your site. If you can add a Google Analytics tag, you can install this. We will do it for you if needed.
What if it says something wrong?+
You approve the entire knowledge base before it goes live. The bot is also told to say it does not know and to contact the business when it lacks info. It will not make things up. You can edit or pause anytime.
How is this different from just using ChatGPT?+
A raw ChatGPT will hallucinate your hours, prices, and services. This one is constrained to only what you approve, so visitors get real answers about your actual business.
Can I see what people are asking?+
Yes. Every conversation is logged. Review them anytime and update your knowledge base to answer common questions better.
