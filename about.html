About Handled

A done-for-you AI automation service for local businesses. No agencies, no project managers, no setup fees buried in fine print. You pay, you get an automation that runs.

What we actually do

Local business owners tell us what they're tired of doing manually. We build the AI automation that handles it. The whole thing is live within 3 business days.

Most of the work falls into a few categories: responding to Google reviews, replying to missed-call texts, following up on quotes that went cold, sending appointment reminders that actually get opened, asking happy customers to leave reviews at the right moment, and answering inbound questions through email or SMS in your voice without you typing every reply.

You don't need to know what an "AI agent" is, what an "API" does, or which platforms we use behind the scenes. You tell us what's eating your week. We build the thing that stops it.

What makes us different

Most "AI automation" companies sell software you have to learn. We don't. Handled is a service. We do the building, the testing, the monitoring, and the fixing. You log in once to authorize a connection. After that, the only signal you'll get that we exist is an inbox that's quieter and a calendar that's fuller.

Who builds this

Handled is operated by Leland McAlmont, a solo developer based in Thornton, Colorado. Background includes hardware engineering at BAE Systems on radar/CAD work, applied AI integration across the small-business stack, and a stubborn belief that the right answer to "how do I save 12 hours a week" is almost never "buy another piece of software."

The technical infrastructure runs on production-grade tools: Stripe for payments, Make.com for workflow orchestration, SendGrid for email, the OpenRouter API for language model inference, Supabase for state. Every automation is monitored. Every customer interaction is logged. When something breaks, we know before you do.

How we make money

One-time setup fees and optional monthly maintenance. That's it. We don't sell your data, we don't take affiliate commissions from the tools we use, we don't run ads, we don't have investors pushing us to upsell things you don't need. You pay us directly. We work for you directly. That's the whole business model.
