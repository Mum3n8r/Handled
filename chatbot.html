FAQ
Straight answers.
Do I need any technical knowledge to install it?+
You copy one <script> tag into your site's HTML. If you can add a Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel tag, you can install this. If not, we'll do it for you.
What if it says something wrong?+
The bot is instructed to say "I don't know, please contact the business" when it doesn't have the info. You approve the entire knowledge base before it goes live, and you can edit or pause anytime.
Can I see what people are asking it?+
Yes. Every conversation is logged. You can review them, spot common questions, and update your knowledge base to answer them better.
What happens if I hit 1,000 conversations?+
We reach out. Most small business sites are nowhere near this. If you're routinely over, we'll bump you to a custom plan - never a surprise charge.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. Monthly billing, no annual contract, cancel and it stops. No cancellation fees or awkward retention emails.
Does it work with WordPress / Squarespace / Wix?+
Yes, all of them. Anywhere you can paste an HTML snippet or embed code, our widget works.
How is this different from ChatGPT on my site?+
A raw ChatGPT will make things up about your business. This one is constrained to what you approve and told to defer to you when it doesn't know. That difference matters when a visitor is deciding whether to book.
